#citystate 

Demonym: Angeli 

**Government Type:** Dictatorship
The land is predominantly covered in frozen wasteland, with polar desert to the North. The weather is usually cold and windy.

**Population:** 58,919,616 (58.9 Million approx) 
(52% male, 48% female)

**Life expectancy:** Male: 66 years, Female: 75 years

**Average children per family:** 3 

**Population Density:** 29% urban, 10% suburban, 61% rural.

**Ethnic Groups:** 88% Indigenous population, 4% Local-origin migrants, 8% Short-term residents

**Languages:** There is 1 official language, with 3 others spoken.  

**Religious Beliefs:** 32% Major religion, 20% Minor religion, 15% Agnostic, 33% Atheist.
# Military 
**Total Military Personnel:** 15,066,562 (25.57% of the total population).

**Active Military:** 30,532,639 (51.82% of the total population).

**Maximum Military Manpower:** 1,626,696 (12.50% of the total population). _This is the total amount of able bodied citizens who could take up arms if necessary, regardless of age, sex and military experience._ 

They take great pride in their military. They have a defensive pact with 6 other countries. 

Every adult is required to keep a weapon ready at home at all times.

# Culture
**Unemployment rate:** 23% of the population is unemployed.
**Poverty rate:** 13% of the population lives in poverty.
**Literacy rate:** 49% of the population can read and write.
**Resources:** Their primary exports are glass and lubricant.

It is known for it's distinctive accent and furniture style, which varies so much that it can lead to mismatched interior decorating. 

They accents are often slurred together, making it hard to understand the language if you do not speak it.

Stereotypes for Angeli are that they are impatient, economical, and rigid.

Their shared cultural aspects with Other Northerners are found in their method of farming, hospitality rituals, clothing, architecture, and cooking style.
