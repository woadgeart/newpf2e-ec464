#citystate
Demonym: Ardenferi 

**Government Type:** Democracy
The weather is usually freezing and windy. Thundersnow is common. 

**Population:** 13,010,560 (13.0 Million approx)  
(31% male, 69% female) 

**Life expectancy:** Male: 46 years, Female: 68 years 
(The Ardenferi human population possesses a gene carried on the Y chromosome that drastically lowers the average lifespan via sped-up aging.)

**Average children per family:** 5 

**Population Density:** 43% urban, 6% suburban, 51% rural. 

**Ethnic Groups:** 93% Indigenous population, 5% Recent colonists, 1% Distant-origin migrants, 1% Refugees 

**Languages:** There is 1 official language.  

**Religious Beliefs:** 1% Major religion, 1% Agnostic, 98% Atheist.
# Military 
**Total Military Personnel:** 326,363 (2.51% of the total population). 

**Active Military:** 62,690 (0.48% of the total population). 

**Maximum Military Manpower:** 1,626,696 (12.50% of the total population). _This is the total amount of able bodied citizens who could take up arms if necessary, regardless of age, sex and military experience._ 

They are seen as an easily offended nation, quick to defend their pride. 
They have a defensive pact with 1 other country ([[1. World Almanac/Yetora/Iceland/City States/Temperford\|Temperford]]). 
Their military equipment is effective. They have mandatory military service for all adults.
# Culture
**Unemployment rate:** 13% of the population is unemployed. 
**Poverty rate:** 22% of the population lives in poverty. 
**Literacy rate:** 91% of the population can read and write. 
**Resources:** Ardenfer has several natural resources, most notably coal and iron.

It is known for it's clothing style, cuisine, music, and distinctive language, all of which are inspired by Slavic traditions. 

They craft intricate footwear designs and are known to be pushing the boundaries when it comes to bootcraft. 

Their food features many vegetables, especially roots. 

Their furniture is sturdy, and their music is often lively. 

Fabrics are known for interweaving patterns here. 

Their language is a tonal language.

Stereotypes for Ardenferi are that they are many beautiful women, but often suspicious of others.

Their shared cultural aspects with Temperford are found in their method of farming, hospitality rituals, and cooking style.
