#citystate
Demonym: Temperfordian 

**Government Type:** Feudal
The land is predominantly covered in volcanically-active land. The weather is usually cold and cloudy.

**Population:** 18,084,870 (18.1 Million approx)  
(54% male, 46% female)

**Life expectancy:** Male: 89 years, Female: 95 years 
(The Temperfordian human population is extraordinarily long-lived.)

**Average children per family:** 6

**Population Density:** 44% urban, 16% suburban, 40% rural.

**Ethnic Groups:** 76% Indigenous population, 19% Former Colonist Descendants, 3% Local-origin migrants, 2% Short-term residents

**Languages:** There is 1 official language, with 2 others spoken.

**Religious Beliefs:** 11% Major religion, 15% Polytheistic, 20% Agnostic, 54% Atheist.
# Military 
**Total Military Personnel:** 286,753 (1.6% of the total population).

**Active Military:** 134,419 (0.74% of the total population).

**Maximum Military Manpower:** 1,565,193 (8.65% of the total population). _This is the total amount of able bodied citizens who could take up arms if necessary, regardless of age, sex and military experience._ 

They are seen as neutral nation.
They have a defensive pact with 2 other countries ([[1. World Almanac/Yetora/Iceland/City States/Ardenfer\|Ardenfer]] and [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/Northlands/City States/Angelforge\|Angelforge]]).
Military service is not mandatory.
# Culture
**Unemployment rate:** 28% of the population is unemployed.
**Poverty rate:** 31% of the population lives in poverty.
**Literacy rate:** 53% of the population can read and write.
**Resources:** Ardenfer has several natural resources, most notably coal and wheat.

The people here value fashion over function in their clothing. The humans here often have blonde hair and blue eyes, and their culture is reminiscent of Nordic cultures.

Their traditional music is choral singing, with holidays often being celebrated by music festivals. 

Their architecture features layered rings.

Their men's traditional hairstyle is close-cropped.

Fabrics are known for being incredibly durable here.

Their language is often incredibly harshly pronounced.

Stereotypes for Temperfordians include being naive and friendly.

Their shared cultural aspects with Ardenfer are found in their method of farming, hospitality rituals, and cooking style.
