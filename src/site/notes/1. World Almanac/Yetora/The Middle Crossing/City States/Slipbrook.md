(53% male, 47% female)

# Military 
**Total Military Personnel:** 23,495,643 (20.43% of the total population). 

**Active Military:** 10,017,830 (8.71% of the total population). 

**Maximum Military Manpower:** 60,160,257 (52.30% of the total population). _This is the total amount of able bodied citizens who could take up arms if necessary, regardless of age, sex and military experience._

They have a defensive pact with 1 other country ([[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Middle Crossing/City States/Westerwich\|Westerwich]]). Their military equipment is out-dated but effective. 

Military service is seen as an essential way of life.

Their clothing often includes vibrant colors.
