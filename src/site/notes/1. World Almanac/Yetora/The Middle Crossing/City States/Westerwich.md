#citystate

Demonym: Wich

**Government Type:** Democracy
The weather is usually cool and still.

**Population:** 138,240,096 (138.2 Million approx)  
(47% male, 53% female) 

**Life expectancy:** Male: 66 years, Female: 70 years 

**Average children per family:** 10 

**Population Density:** 53% urban, 36% suburban, 11% rural. 

**Ethnic Groups:** 80% Ancient settlers, 2% Recent settlers, 8% Local and distant-origin immigrants, 10% Refugees 

**Languages:** There is 1 official language, with 1 others spoken.  

**Religious Beliefs:** 3% Major religion, 68% Minor religion, 23% Agnostic, 6% Atheist.

# Military 
**Total Military Personnel:** 855,551 (0.62% of the total population). 

**Active Military:** 355,527 (0.26% of the total population). 

**Maximum Military Manpower:** 3,749,143 (2.71% of the total population). _This is the total amount of able bodied citizens who could take up arms if necessary, regardless of age, sex and military experience._ 

They frequently attack neighbouring regions. They have a defensive pact with 1 other country ([[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Middle Crossing/City States/Slipbrook\|Slipbrook]]). Their military equipment is average. Military service is seen as a viable career path.
# Culture
**Unemployment rate:** 17% of the population is unemployed. 
**Poverty rate:** 17% of the population lives in poverty. 
**Literacy rate:** 44% of the population can read and write.

**Resources:** They do not export, but rather exert pressure on international trade as a middleman.

Wich music lacks any real rhythm.  
For Wich men, shoulder length hair is a practical and stylish choice.  
The Wich art movement places a strong emphasis on minimalism and simplicity.  
Their people are often stereotyped as being honourable.
