#region
The Middle Crossing is an economically significant zone on the continent, being the widest opening in the central mountains that marks the separation of the [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/Northlands/Regional Markers/Northern Mountain Range\|Northern Mountain Range]] and [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Middle Crossing/Regional Markers/The Southern Mountains\|The Southern Mountains]]. 

It is home to the twin city-states, [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Middle Crossing/City States/Slipbrook\|Slipbrook]] and [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Middle Crossing/City States/Westerwich\|Westerwich]], as well as the township of [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Middle Crossing/Townships/Dark Pass\|Dark Pass]] which sits between them. Almost all trade between the East and West passes through the Middle Crossing.

It is bordered by [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Verdant Groves/The Verdant Groves\|The Verdant Groves]] to the East, and [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Far West/Regional Markers/The Western Drought\|The Western Drought]] to the West.
