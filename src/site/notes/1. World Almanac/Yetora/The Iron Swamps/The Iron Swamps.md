#region

The Iron Swamps is a marshy region in the Southern half of the continent, stretching from [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Lesser Forest/The Lesser Forest\|The Lesser Forest]] to the [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/Snowland/Snowland\|Snowland]] strait. It is home to the city-state of [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Iron Swamps/City State/Ironmill\|Ironmill]], and it's three satellite port towns, [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Iron Swamps/Townships/Rosenspell\|Rosenspell]], [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Iron Swamps/Townships/Decastop\|Decastop]], and [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Iron Swamps/Townships/Mortarleigh\|Mortarleigh]].
