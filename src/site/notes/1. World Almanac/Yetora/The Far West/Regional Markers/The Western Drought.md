#region

The Western Drought is a massive dry region stretching from [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Far West/Regional Markers/The Pale Expanse\|The Pale Expanse]] to the south, [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/Iceland/Iceland\|Iceland]] in the North, the Western Ocean, and the [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/Northlands/Regional Markers/Northern Mountain Range\|Northern Mountain Range]] and [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Middle Crossing/The Middle Crossing\|The Middle Crossing]] to the East. It ranges from Tundra in the far north to savannah in the center, and desert in the south.
