#region

The Pale Expanse is a large desert taking up most of the Southwestern section of the continent, bordered by [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Middle Crossing/Regional Markers/The Southern Mountains\|The Southern Mountains]] (and [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Lesser Forest/Deepsuth Mont\|Deepsuth Mont]]), and [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Far West/Regional Markers/The Western Drought\|The Western Drought]] to the North. It rests along the coast of the Western Ocean. It's culture is dominated by the Arab-inspired Azure Sultanate.
