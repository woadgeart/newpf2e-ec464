#citystate

Demonym: White

**Government Type:** Democracy
The weather is usually warm and sunny. Droughts are common.

**Population:** 2,295,272 (2.3 Million approx)  
(47% male, 53% female) 

**Life expectancy:** Male: 89 years, Female: 58 years 

**Average children per family:** 3 

**Population Density:** 59% urban, 21% suburban, 20% rural. 

**Ethnic Groups:** 99% Natives, 1% Refugees 

**Languages:** There is 1 official language, with 1 others spoken.  

**Religious Beliefs:** 64% Major religion, 27% Pagan, 1% Agnostic, 8% Atheist.

# Military 
**Total Military Personnel:** 42,704 (1.9% of the total population). 

**Active Military:** 12,329 (0.54% of the total population). 

**Maximum Military Manpower:** 251,720 (10.97% of the total population). _This is the total amount of able bodied citizens who could take up arms if necessary, regardless of age, sex and military experience._ 

They are not quick to attack others but will defend themselves and their allies when necessary. 

Their military equipment is poorly maintained. 

They have mandatory military service for all adult males.

# Culture
**Unemployment rate:** 20% of the population is unemployed. 
**Poverty rate:** 37% of the population lives in poverty. 
**Literacy rate:** 34% of the population can read and write.
**Resources:** Their primary export is Crops and Cattle.

Whitefer is well known for its building style, music style, textiles, vocal style, art style, clothing style, instruments, and distinctive language

They make prominent use of symmetry in their building designs.  

White music lacks any real rhythm.  

White fabrics are known for intricate weaving patterns that captivate the eye.  

Their songs are very catchy.  

Their art often explores themes of social justice and activism.  

They usually go naked, or wear very little clothing; their culture never developed a shame complex around nudity.  

Instruments like flutes and stringed instruments are popular in White music.  

Their language boasts a unique script or writing system.  

Their people are often stereotyped as being physically attractive, responsible, and vulgar.
