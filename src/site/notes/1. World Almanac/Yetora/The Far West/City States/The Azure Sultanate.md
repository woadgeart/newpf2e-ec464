#citystate

Demonym: Azurean 

**Government Type:** Monarchy
The weather is usually warm and sunny. Heat waves are common.

**Population:** 737,750 (738 Thousand approx)  
(54% male, 46% female) 

**Life expectancy:** Male: 57 years, Female: 86 years 

**Average children per family:** 2

**Population Density:** 70% urban, 17% suburban, 13% rural. 

**Ethnic Groups:** 95% Ancient settlers, 1% Recent settlers, 4% Refugees

**Languages:** There is 1 official language, with 1 others spoken.  

**Religious Beliefs:** 81% Major religion, 4% Minor religion, 9% Agnostic, 6% Atheist.

# Military 
**Total Military Personnel:** 5,119 (0.69% of the total population). 

**Active Military:** 2,314 (0.31% of the total population). 

**Maximum Military Manpower:** 21,946 (2.97% of the total population). _This is the total amount of able bodied citizens who could take up arms if necessary, regardless of age, sex and military experience._

# Culture
**Unemployment rate:** 10% of the population is unemployed. 
**Poverty rate:** 3% of the population lives in poverty. 
**Literacy rate:** 95% of the population can read and write.
**Resources:** Their primary export is Salt, Emeralds, and Copper.

The Azureans are well known for their distinctive accent, distinctive language, building style, music style, vocal style, art style, clothing style, and textiles --- Their speech patterns can be challenging for outsiders to mimic.  

Their language sounds very similar to a distant, unrelated language.  

Efficient space utilization is a hallmark of their cities and buildings.  

Azurean music is a source of cultural pride.  

Their language makes their singing sound very dramatic, even when it isn't.  

Their art is heavily influenced by nature and wildlife.  

Men and women do not have distinctive clothes.  

Azurean fabrics stand out for their long-lasting quality and durability.  

Their people are often stereotyped as being kind, and pious.

The nation is surrounded by a massive border wall, manned with sentries. It is nigh-impenetrable, though travelers and traders are often permitted in.
