#citystate

Demonym: Equin 

**Government Type:** Fascist Dictatorship

*Fascist Traits:*
- Powerful and Continuing Nationalism
- Authoritarianism and Leader Cult
- Supremacy of the Military 
- Identification of enemies as a unifying cause (Monsters in the wilderness, Tribespeople to the South) 
- Disdain for Human Rights 
- Controlled Media and Propaganda 
- Religion and Government Intertwined 
- Suppression of Labor and Protection of Corporate Power 
- Contempt for Intellectuals and the Arts 
- Obsession with Crime and Punishment 
- Rampant Corruption 

The weather is usually hot and sunny for prolonged periods of time.

**Population:** 1,672,587 (1.7 Million approx)  
(47% male, 53% female) 

**Life expectancy:** Male: 102 years, Female: 114 years (their population is primarily half-elven due to generations of interbreeding between the local elven tribes and humans).

**Average children per family:** 3 

**Population Density:** 82% urban, 13% suburban, 5% rural. 

**Ethnic Groups:** 64% Ancient settlers, 4% Former Colonist Descendants, 8% Distant-origin migrants, 24% Nomadic peoples 

**Languages:** There are 2 official languages, with 1 others spoken.  

**Religious Beliefs:** 91% Major religion, 5% Pagan, 1% Agnostic, 3% Atheist.

# Military 
**Total Military Personnel:** 11,817 (0.71% of the total population). 

**Active Military:** 3,990 (0.24% of the total population). 

**Maximum Military Manpower:** 63,277 (3.78% of the total population). This is the total amount of able bodied citizens who could take up arms if necessary, regardless of age, sex and military experience. 

They are seen as a pacifistic nation. 

They have a defensive pact with 2 other countries ([[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Far West/City States/The Azure Sultanate\|The Azure Sultanate]], [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Far West/City States/Whitefer\|Whitefer]], [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/Snowland/Southheim\|Southheim]]). 

Their military equipment is poorly maintained. 

They have mandatory military service for all adult males.
# Culture
**Unemployment rate:** 5% of the population is unemployed. 
**Poverty rate:** 14% of the population lives in poverty. 
**Literacy rate:** 84% of the population can read and write.
**Resources:** Their primary exports are Livestock, Glass, and Grain Crops.

They are well known for their Renaissance era clothing, their vocal style which features excessive vocal fry, distinctive accent, furniture and building styles.

Equin food features a lot of seafood.  
The Equin people often sport long, flowing hair.  
The Equin building style is decorative.  
Equin music is a source of cultural pride.  
Their people are often stereotyped as being romantic, selfish, and impatient, and a bit strict in their presentation. Outsiders often also perceive them as racist to their neighbors, and zealous in their desire to hunt monsters.
