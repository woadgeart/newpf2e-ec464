# Military 
**Total Military Personnel:** 136 (0.51% of the total population).

**Active Military:** 45 (0.17% of the total population).

**Maximum Military Manpower:** 667 (2.50% of the total population). This is the total amount of able bodied citizens who could take up arms if necessary, regardless of age, sex and military experience.

They have voluntary military service for adults.
# Culture
**Unemployment rate:** 30% of the population is unemployed.
**Poverty rate:** 32% of the population lives in poverty.
**Literacy rate:** 47% of the population can read and write.
**Resources:** Their primary exports are natural fibers and lumber.

Southerners all wear decorative veils, regardless of gender. Their language is very expressive. They produce world-class seafood. 

Their people are often stereotyped as evil, ambitious, and physically unimposing.
