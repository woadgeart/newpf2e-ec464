(54% male, 46% female)

# Culture
**Unemployment rate:** 15% of the population is unemployed.
**Poverty rate:** 21% of the population lives in poverty. 
**Literacy rate:** 43% of the population can read and write. 
**Resources:** Their primary exports are ships and fruit, though they make most of their gold as a port.

They are very far behind technologically.

Their towns are filled with colorful and well painted murals and frescos. Their furniture shows a deep appreciation for craftsmanship. They are stereotyped as being ugly, odd, and over-excited.
