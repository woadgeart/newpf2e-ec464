# Military 
**Total Military Personnel:** 114,318,632 (2.8% of the total population). 
**Active Military:** 31,614,886 (0.78% of the total population). 
**Maximum Military Manpower:** 489,734,135 (12.11% of the total population). _This is the total amount of able bodied citizens who could take up arms if necessary, regardless of age, sex and military experience._

Military service is seen as a viable career path.

# Culture
**Unemployment rate:** 4% of the population is unemployed. 
**Poverty rate:** 7% of the population lives in poverty.
**Literacy rate:** 97% of the population can read and write.

**Resources:** Their primary exports are gold, diamond, sulfur, and bronze.

Afros and natural curls are celebrated in Mysti hair culture.  
Street food vendors are a common sight.  
Mysti music is very discordant.  
The Mysti art movement places a strong emphasis on expression and loose brushstrokes.  
They have a very drawled accent.  
Their singers use many musical scales and complicated singing techniques.  
Traditional Mysti architecture features ornate carvings and intricate details.  
Stringed instruments, such as the lute and lyre, are commonly used in Mysti music.  
Their people are often stereotyped as being apprehensive, and reliable.
