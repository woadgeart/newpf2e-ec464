#region
The Verdant Groves is a massive temperate forest that stretches from [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Great Forest/The Great Forest\|The Great Forest]] in the North, [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Lesser Forest/The Lesser Forest\|The Lesser Forest]] in the South, the Eastern Ocean to the East, and [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Middle Crossing/The Middle Crossing\|The Middle Crossing]], [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/Northlands/Regional Markers/Northern Mountain Range\|Northern Mountain Range]], and [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Middle Crossing/Regional Markers/The Southern Mountains\|The Southern Mountains]] to the West. It is the second largest region on the continent by area, with the first being the Western Drought. 

It is home to two city states; [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Verdant Groves/Amberquay\|Amberquay]] and [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Verdant Groves/Mystpines\|Mystpines]].
