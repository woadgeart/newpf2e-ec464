#citystate

Demonym: Spellrigid

**Government Type:** Mageocracy
The weather is usually hot and still. Rain storms frequently soak the land.

**Population:** 6,031,108,845 (6.0 Billion approx)  
(51% male, 49% female)
**Life expectancy:** Male: 60 years, Female: 69 years 
**Average children per family:** 10 
**Population Density:** 27% urban, 19% suburban, 54% rural. 
**Ethnic Groups:** 90% Indigenous population, 2% Recent settlers, 6% Local and distant-origin immigrants, 2% Refugees 
**Languages:** There is 1 official language, with 2 others spoken.  
**Religious Beliefs:** 96% Major religion, 3% Minor religion, 1% Atheist.

# Military 
**Total Military Personnel:** 405,943,001 (6.73% of the total population). 

**Active Military:** 155,117,075 (2.57% of the total population). 

**Maximum Military Manpower:** 1,021,333,655 (16.93% of the total population). _This is the total amount of able bodied citizens who could take up arms if necessary, regardless of age, sex and military experience._ 

They are seen as an easily offended nation, quick to defend their pride. 

Their military equipment is modern but poorly maintained. 

Military service is not mandatory except during times of war.

# Culture
**Unemployment rate:** 6% of the population is unemployed. 
**Poverty rate:** 22% of the population lives in poverty. 
**Literacy rate:** 80% of the population can read and write.

**Resources:** Their primary exports are court mages and magic knowledge. Other nations pay dues to them to maintain a well trained court mage in their circles.

Cultural symbols are integrated into their cities/buildings.  

Drumming plays a central role in Spellrigid musical performances.  
Their clothing adapts to local climate and geography.  
Their language generally does not rhyme.  
Spellrigid music is very funky.  
Their furniture is very contemporary.
