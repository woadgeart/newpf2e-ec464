#region

The Spellwrought Jungles are a magically volatile region on the continent, being where most [[Ley Lines\|Ley Lines]] converge as a result of it sitting on a tectonic intersection. It is a tropical rainforest with only one city state, and one port town; [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Spellwrought Jungles/City States/Spellridge\|Spellridge]] and [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Spellwrought Jungles/Townships/Warmglade\|Warmglade]], respectively. It is bordered by the [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/Snowland/Snowland\|Snowland]] strait to the South, and [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Iron Swamps/The Iron Swamps\|The Iron Swamps]] to the Northwest, though it is separated from most of the rest of the continent by massive mountains known as the [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Spellwrought Jungles/Regional Markers/Spellridge Walls\|Spellridge Walls]]; there is a gap in the mountains known as the [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Spellwrought Jungles/Regional Markers/Spellstrait\|Spellstrait]].
