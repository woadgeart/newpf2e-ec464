**Government Type:** Republic
The weather is usually cool and sunny.

**Population:** 347,670 (348 Thousand approx)  
(51% male, 49% female) 
**Life expectancy:** Male: 89 years, Female: 106 years
**Average children per family:** 5 
**Population Density:** 72% urban, 2% suburban, 26% rural.
**Ethnic Groups:** 95% Ancient settlers, 3% Distant-origin migrants, 2% Refugees **Languages:** There is 1 official language, with 3 others spoken.  
**Religious Beliefs:** 16% Major religion, 9% Minor religion, 21% Agnostic, 54% Atheist.
# Military 
**Total Military Personnel:** 2,218 (0.64% of the total population). 
**Active Military:** 937 (0.27% of the total population).
**Maximum Military Manpower:** 10,417 (3.00% of the total population). _This is the total amount of able bodied citizens who could take up arms if necessary, regardless of age, sex and military experience._ 
They are seen as weak nation. 
Their military equipment is modern but ineffective. 
They have voluntary military service for adults.

# Culture
**Unemployment rate:** 20% of the population is unemployed. 
**Poverty rate:** 27% of the population lives in poverty. 
**Literacy rate:** 59% of the population can read and write.
**Resources:** Their primary export is Lubricant and Armor.

Taloncliffe is well known for its textiles, art style, cuisine, clothing style, hair styles, furniture style, and vocal style.

Talon fabrics often feature distinctive handwoven textures.  
They create excellent portraits.  
Talon food features a lot of hunted meat.  
They craft intricate cloak designs.  
The Talon people only cut their hair if they are forced to leave Talon.  
Their furniture features no patterns but is brightly coloured.  
Their folk songs often tell stories of local legends and history.  
Their people are often stereotyped as being stupid, and weak.
