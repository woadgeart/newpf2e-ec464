World Almanac/Yetora/Northlands/Regional Markers/Northern Mountain Range\|Northern Mountain Range]] to the Eastern Ocean, and from the Northernmost part of the continent down to [[1. World Almanac/Yetora/The Verdant Groves/The Verdant Groves\|The Verdant Groves]]. It is considered to be in the [[1.
